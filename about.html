About SASTRA

SASTRA Deemed University was established in the year 1984 as Shanmugha College of Engineering at a rural village Tirumalaisamudram in Thanjavur District. The University offers various UG, PG & Ph. D. programmes in Engineering, Science, Management, Law, and Arts. It is recognized as a Scientific & Industrial Research Organization (SIRO) by the Government of India and is undertaking research for various agencies like DST, DBT, AYUSH, DRDO, DRDL, ISRO, BRNS, etc. SASTRA is re-accredited (III Cycle) by the National Assessment & Accreditation Council (NAAC) with an A+ grade and also ranked amongst the top universities by the Government of India through NIRF and other global ranking agencies like Times Higher Education World & Asia, QS BRICS, QS Asia & India, Times Higher Education Emerging Economies University Rankings, etc. SASTRA is recognized as a Category-1 University as per the UGC’s Graded Autonomy Regulations, 2018. SASTRA is also actively involved in various social & community development projects for the betterment & upliftment of various people from differently challenged environments.

About School Of Management(SOM)

The School of Management was established in Year 1998 and has developed itself into a Centre of Excellence. The School offers the following programmes:

  • Master of Business Administrator[MBA]
    (Accredited by TCS)
    Duration: Two Year[4 semesters]
  • B.com-Computer Application[B.com-CA]
    Duration: Three Years[6 semesters]
  • B.com-BFSI
    Duration: Three Years[6 semesters]
  • Doctral Programme - Ph.D

The academic learning at SOM is anchored around the social and culture-specific context of management, and hence, the focus is on experiential learning. The School has strong industry affiliations with many leading companies visiting the campus for placement. The program structure and pedagogy require students to experience what they learn and undertake specially designed tasks where they apply what they learn and evaluate their learning. The purpose is to “involve” and “engage” students in the process of learning. About ICFODSED -2022

The School of Management is organizing the “ International Conference on Fostering Digital Transformation for Sustainable Socio-Economic Development ”- (ICFODSED -2022). The Conference will feature an objective demonstration of various ideas through technology and applications of contemporary digital transformation for sustainable socio-economic development. The schedule of the conference is designed over phygital format for purposeful discourse with resources across the globe. The specific objectives of this conference are :

  • To capture successful digital disruption that led the transformation across economic, social, and business functions.
  • To validate this transformation in terms of their value addition and sustainability amidst the future challenges.
  • To explore strategic alternatives for building a digitally empowered and economically advanced society.

The Conference will provide an ideal platform for the academicians, researchers, and representatives from the industry to present the key milestones of their research pursuits and intended outcomes.
